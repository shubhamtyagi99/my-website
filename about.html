About Ardaas Cables

Ardaas Cables Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of PTFE, FEP, PVC insulated wires and cables, dedicated to providing high quality solutions for various industries. We specialize in creating durable and reliable wires and cables designed to withstand extreme temperatures and demanding environments.

Our products including high temperature wires, flexible cables and custom designed solutions serve critical applications in sectors like aerospace, defence and electronics.

We are committed to excellence from sourcing premium raw material to utilizing advanced manufacturing processes, ensuring every product meets rigorous quality standards.

We pride ourselves on building strong relationships with a diverse clientele, delivering reliable high-performance wiring solutions tailored to their needs.
